About Our Temple

Rooted in Ancient Tradition, Serving Modern Devotees

Our Sacred History

Sri Chaitanya Chandra Mandir stands as a devoted outpost of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), established to serve the spiritual needs of the devotees and community in Kalyani, Nadia. Our temple was founded with the vision of creating a sacred space where the teachings of Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu could flourish.

Named after the golden moon-like effulgence of Lord Chaitanya, our temple has been a center of devotional activities, bringing together souls seeking spiritual growth and divine connection through the practice of bhakti yoga.

Our Spiritual Lineage (Parampara)

We are blessed to be part of the Brahma-Madhva-Gaudiya Sampradaya, a disciplic succession that traces back to Lord Krishna Himself. Our connection to ISKCON ensures that we maintain the pure teachings as given by:

Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu

The Golden Avatar who taught love of God through kirtan

Srila Bhaktisiddhanta Sarasvati

The great preacher of Gaudiya Vaishnavism

A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

Founder-Acharya of ISKCON

Through this unbroken chain of spiritual masters, we receive and share the pure devotional teachings that transform hearts and awaken divine love.
